مرحباً بكم في معهد تلندينج الإسلامي

يخدم معهد تلندينج الإسلامي المجتمع لسنوات، حيث يوفر تعليمًا إسلاميًا عالي الجودة مقترنًا بالتميز الأكاديمي. تأسست مؤسستنا على أساس القيم الإسلامية، وتعزز الانضباط والاحترام والنزاهة الأخلاقية بين طلابنا.

نحن ملتزمون برعاية العقول الشابة بالمعرفة الدينية والتعليم الحديث على حد سواء، مما يضمن نمو طلابنا ليصبحوا أعضاء مسؤولين ومتعلمين ومؤمنين في المجتمع.

OUR MISSION

  • Promoting Islamic morals and values in all aspects of education
  • Fostering academic growth and intellectual development
  • Nurturing responsible citizenship and community engagement
  • Providing a safe and supportive learning environment

OUR VISION

To be a leading Islamic educational institution that produces well-rounded individuals who excel academically, embody Islamic values, and contribute positively to society.

We envision a future where our graduates are recognized for their knowledge, character, and commitment to serving their communities with wisdom and compassion.

Through continuous improvement and dedication to excellence, we strive to maintain our position as a trusted institution for Islamic education in The Gambia.

About Our Institute

Tallinding Islamic Institution has been serving the community for years, providing quality Islamic education combined with academic excellence. Our institution is built on the foundation of Islamic values, promoting discipline, respect, and moral integrity among our students

Founded in 1963, we continue to uphold our commitment to excellence in Islamic education
